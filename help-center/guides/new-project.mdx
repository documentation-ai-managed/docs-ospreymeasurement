## Overview

Projects organize your measurement data.

## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to dashboard">
    Visit `https://ospreymeasurement.com/dashboard` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    Click "Projects" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create project">
    Press "New Project", enter a name, and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload data (optional)">
    Add measurement files or connect a device.
  </Step>
</Steps>
