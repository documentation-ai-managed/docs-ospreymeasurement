<Card title="Worldsensing Output" href="https://ospreymeasurement.github.io/OspreyDocs/apps/asp-explorer/index.html" horizontal="false" target="_self">
  This tool allows you to chart ASP data direct from the Worldsensing Digital Node:



  1. .Drag-and-drop or navigate to your data file.&#x20;

  2. Choose which sensor to use as the reference sensor.

  3. Click on a measurement point to use that timestamp as the baseline.

  4. Use the slider bar at the bottom to adjust the period shown.
