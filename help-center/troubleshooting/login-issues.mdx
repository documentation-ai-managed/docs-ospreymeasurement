---
title: Can't log in to dashboard
description: Fix dashboard login problems.
---

## Symptoms

- Can't access dashboard
- Error: "Invalid credentials" or "Account locked"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Request a reset at `ospreymeasurement.com/reset-password`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cookies and reload the login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    Email `support@ospreymeasurement.com` if still stuck.
  </Step>
</Steps>
